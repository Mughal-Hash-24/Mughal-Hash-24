## About Me

I'm a Computer Science undergrad at **FAST-NUCES (Batch '28)** based in Pakistan, passionate about building things at the intersection of AI, systems programming, and interactive software.

I work across a wide range — from training LLMs and building RAG pipelines, to writing low-level assembly and shipping games with C++/SFML. I learn best by building, and I'm always working on something new.

Currently, I'm also the **founder & CEO of a startup** focused on workflow automation — turning repetitive processes into intelligent, automated systems.
